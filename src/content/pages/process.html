Personalized Care Tailored to Your Needs

Treatment Approaches You Can Expect

Dr.Cohen uses a variety of techniques to relieve pain, restore mobility, and enhance your overall well-being. His treatment approaches are designed to address not just symptoms, but the underlying causes of discomfort for long-lasting relief. Whether it’s through spinal adjustments, therapeutic exercises, or lifestyle guidance, our care is personalized to fit your needs. Learn more about the treatment methods you can expect below.

  • Spinal Adjustments

    Spinal adjustments are the cornerstone of chiropractic care. By applying controlled force to specific joints in the spine, chiropractors restore proper alignment, reduce nerve irritation, and improve mobility. This non-invasive technique provides relief from pain caused by misalignments or tension.

  • Therapeutic Modalities

    Chiropractors often use therapeutic modalities such as electric muscle stimulation (EMS), ultrasound, and heat or cold therapy. These techniques enhance circulation, reduce inflammation, relieve muscle tension, and promote healing in soft tissues, providing an added layer of pain management.

  • Traction Therapy

    Traction therapy gently stretches the spine to relieve pressure on discs and nerves. This treatment is especially effective for conditions like herniated discs, spinal stenosis, or pinched nerves. It helps create space for healing and alleviates symptoms like pain, numbness, and tingling.

  • Therapeutic Exercises

    Chiropractors design personalized therapeutic exercise programs to strengthen supporting muscles, improve flexibility, and stabilize the spine. These exercises complement in-office treatments, reduce the risk of future injuries, and support long-term spinal health.

  • Posture and Ergonomic Coaching

    Poor posture and improper ergonomics often contribute to spinal issues. Chiropractors provide guidance on improving posture and recommend ergonomic adjustments for daily activities, such as sitting at a desk or lifting objects. These changes help prevent recurring pain and promote overall wellness

  • Holistic Lifestyle Guidance

    Chiropractors take a whole-body approach to care, offering advice on nutrition, hydration, stress management, and sleep hygiene. These lifestyle adjustments enhance the body’s natural healing processes and complement chiropractic treatments, helping patients achieve optimal health.
